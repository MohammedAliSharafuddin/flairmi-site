Unification Rules

  • FlairMI is the house brand and canonical public URL system.
  • Product repos stay separate when the codebase, release cadence, or install path is different.
  • Free utilities support discovery under Tools, not as disconnected microsites.
  • Books and courseware live under Press and Teaching, not as isolated project brands.
  • Benchmark repos, forks, and abandoned prototypes move to Labs or stay private.

Current Focus

Immediate goal. Make flairmi the source of truth again, then point every public repo and landing page back to these canonical routes.
  • Sync public-only assets and copy from flairmi-site into flairmi.
  • Replace scattered suite placeholders with product and press architecture.
  • Preserve legacy /stats, /survey, and /writer routes as continuity pages while the product surface is consolidated.
  • Move books and courseware into explicit FlairMI Press positioning.
  • Track merge, archive, and benchmark repos in one internal TODO map.
